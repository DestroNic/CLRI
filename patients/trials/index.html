Clinical Trials

What are Clinical Trials?

Clinical trials are research programs that use volunteers in order to:

Expand the knowledge on disease process.

Find better or new treatments.

Determine safety and efficacy of novel interventions.

Pre-Screening Visit and FibroScan

A pre-screening visit is conducted to evaluate your eligibility to participate in a clinical trial.


It consists of getting your medical history, performing a FibroScan and sometimes doing blood work.


This information will allow us to determine if you will qualify to participate in a clinical trial.
