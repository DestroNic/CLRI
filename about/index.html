About Us

Our Mission

California Liver Research Institute is a non-profit organization focused on the advancement of treatment and management of liver diseases. Our commitment is to expand and improve the knowledge of liver diseases and novel therapies, as well as to deliver quality patient care.

Here are some useful facts

CLRI is a research facility that conducts clinical trials on liver disease.

We pride ourselves in the experience of our professionals and the quality of care offered to our patients.

As a single center, the average time to start up a study is shorter, and your will get timely responses to your inquiries

On site we have capacity to store frozen samples, centrifuge, FibroScan and laboratory.
